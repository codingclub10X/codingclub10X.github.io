Welcome to the 10X Coding Club

Vision: To empower future innovators to make a meaningful impact through collaboration and technology.

About Us

Mission:

  • Our mission is to work together with members of the club on problem-solving projects related to AI and Web Development, conduct computer programming lessons with IICS students, and build coding skills in students to make them future-ready.

Activities:

  • Collaboration on projects that have a real-world impact (1 AI-themed project and 1 Web Development project)
  • Conducting periodical lessons on computer literacy and basic programming for IICS students
  • Preparation for and participation in hackathons/competitions as a team (representing 10X)

Objectives:

  • To foster an environment that improves students’ collaboration, programming, critical and computational thinking, and analytical skills.
  • To inspire creativity and innovation
  • To make students future-ready
