About Us

Mission:
Our mission is to work together with members of the club on problem-solving projects related to AI and Web Development, conduct computer programming lessons with IICS students, and build coding skills in students to make them future-ready.

Activities:

  • Collaboration on projects that have a real-world impact (1 AI-themed project and 1 Web Development project)
  • Conducting periodical lessons on computer literacy and basic programming for IICS students
  • Preparation for and participation in hackathons/competitions as a team (representing 10X)

Project Showcase

Project 1: AI-Powered Chatbot

Our team developed an AI-powered chatbot that helps students with their coding assignments by providing instant feedback and suggestions. The chatbot is built using natural language processing techniques and is integrated into our club's website for easy access.

Project 2: Web Development Portfolio

Members of our club created a portfolio website that showcases their web development skills. The site features various projects, including responsive designs, interactive elements, and custom-built components.

Project 3: Mobile App Development

Our club members collaborated on a mobile app that helps users track their daily coding progress. The app includes features such as goal setting, progress tracking, and motivational reminders to keep users on track with their learning.
